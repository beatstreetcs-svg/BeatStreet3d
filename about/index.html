About BeatStreet 3D

Dance, dreams, determination.

BeatStreet 3D is a Margate dance studio built around energetic instruction, student confidence, and a performance-minded studio community.

Explore classes

Studio philosophy

A place where dancers can grow.

BeatStreet 3D serves families in Margate, Coconut Creek, Coral Springs, and surrounding Broward communities. The studio supports dancers through technique, choreography, performance opportunities, and a culture of encouragement.

Whether a child is trying dance for the first time or preparing for competitive team training, the goal is the same: help dancers feel strong, expressive, prepared, and proud of their progress.

Teaching team

Warm coaching. Serious craft.

The BeatStreet 3D staff focuses on fundamentals, confidence, and the kind of studio habits that help dancers stay excited about training.
