Hip-hop classes

Kids hip-hop classes near Coral Springs

Hip-hop is a popular choice for kids who love music, rhythm, expressive movement, and high-energy choreography.

What students practice in hip-hop

Kids hip-hop classes often include warmups, rhythm drills, choreography, formations, musicality, and performance skills. Dancers learn how to move with confidence while listening closely to counts and music changes.

Why hip-hop builds confidence

Because hip-hop is expressive and energetic, it can help dancers become more comfortable taking up space, remembering choreography, and performing in front of others.

A nearby option for Coral Springs families
